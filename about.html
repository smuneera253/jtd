Mission & Vision Alignment

At JTD, actions and initiatives are deeply rooted in our Grand Mission, setting a clear, purpose driven direction for the organization. Each program within JTD is envisioned with a 10 year horizon, encapsulating our aspirations and the impact we earn to achieve, aligning with our overarching mission and vision.

Quarterly OKRs

The transition from annual strategy to actionable steps is marked by our commitement to OKRs. In the last week of each quarter, Program Owners craft Objectives and Key Results for the forthcoming quarter, drawing directly from the annual strategy. This systematic approach ensures our goals are specific, measurable, and aligned across the organization, driving focus and clarity.nts that improve

Annual Strategy

Our annual strategy formulation is an exercise of precision and foresight, employing Richard Rumelt's Strategy Template in a dedicated workshop. This process begins at the organizationallevel, setting a strategic framework that each Program Owner then adapts to develop a tailored strategy for their respective programs, ensuring all efforts are in harmony with our grand vision.

Montly Sprints

The execution of our Key Results is propelled by monthly sprints, with sprint goals meticulously derived to advance our quarterly objectives. Adopting best practices like weekly one-on-ones and scrum ceremonies, we ensure a smooth, agile journey towards our strategic goals, ironing out any obstacles along the way for seamless progress.
